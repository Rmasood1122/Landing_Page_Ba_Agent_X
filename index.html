FAQ

Good to know

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?

Yes — Stripe requires a card to start a trial, but you won't be charged until the 14 days are up, and you can cancel anytime before then from your account.

Can another company see our data?

No. Every workspace is isolated at the database level — every request is automatically scoped to your company, with no shared access between tenants.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, reach out to support and we'll move you between plans — your data and users stay exactly where they are.

What happens if a payment fails?

We'll retry automatically and email you. Access pauses only if a subscription lapses fully — your data is never deleted.
